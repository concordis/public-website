About Concordis’ Founders

…and why it’s time for this refreshing approach.

After 30 years of collective experience, we saw a need to bring transparency to the staffing industry and focus on creating a company that treats people the way we would want to be treated. This simple Golden Rule foundation helped us found Concordis and create a symbiotic relationship with our clients and candidates. We believe in hinging our success on the success of everyone involved.
